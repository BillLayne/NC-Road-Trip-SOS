Your insurance for your house only protects your stuff a little bit when you travel. And if you rent out your place (like on Airbnb), it probably won't protect you if a guest gets hurt.

Stuff Stolen From Your Car

If someone steals your laptop and tablet from your car, your homeowners insurance might only pay a tiny part of what they're worth after your deductible.

The Airbnb Problem

If you rent your house to someone and they get hurt, your normal insurance will probably say "not our problem," and you could be sued for a lot of money.

Quick Fix:

  • Check your homeowners policy to see how much it covers for things stolen outside your home.
  • If you rent your place, you need special "short-term rental" insurance. Call your agent ASAP.
  • Ask about an "umbrella policy." It's extra protection for big lawsuits and doesn't cost much.
